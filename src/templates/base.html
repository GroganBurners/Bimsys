Welcome to Grogan Burners Online

Grogan Burner Services Ltd. is an independent heating appliance service company based in Kilkenny serving the South East of Ireland. We have over 40 years of experience in the heating business. We install, commission, service and repair most makes of oil and gas fired boilers, fires etc.

Why service your boiler?

Sustainable Energy Ireland (S.E.I) maintain you can save €150 on your fuel bill every year by having your boiler serviced. Also all boiler manufacturers recommend the best way to ensure your boiler runs safely, reliably and efficiently is to have it serviced annually. An efficient boiler means reduced risk of carbon monoxide generation, lower oil and gas usage (saving you €€€) and a more environmentally friendly boiler due to reduced carbon dioxide emissions.

We are also certified Building Energy Rating (BER) assessors.

From 1st Jan 2009 all dwellings for sale or rent in Ireland need to have a building energy rating or BER completed. Fines of up to €5000 are being imposed by sustainable energy Ireland for non compliance.
